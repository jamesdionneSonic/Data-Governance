# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and collaborative environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent.
- Give actionable, technical feedback.
- Focus on code and outcomes, not people.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Disruptive behavior in reviews or discussions.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict access for violations.

## Reporting

Report concerns to project maintainers through a private channel.
