# Data Steward

## Definition

A **Data Steward** is the individual responsible for the day-to-day management and quality
of a data domain, ensuring that data definitions, metadata, and quality standards are upheld.

## Responsibilities

- Maintain business glossary definitions within their domain
- Approve data quality rules and exceptions
- Review and approve access requests for sensitive data
- Coordinate with technical custodians on schema changes
- Ensure classification labels are accurate and up-to-date

## Difference from Data Owner

| Role           | Focus                           | Accountability            |
| -------------- | ------------------------------- | ------------------------- |
| Data Owner     | Strategic, business value       | Executive accountability  |
| Data Steward   | Operational, day-to-day quality | Functional accountability |
| Data Custodian | Technical, storage & access     | IT accountability         |

## Governance Implications

Data Stewards must be assigned to every data domain. Unstewardship of data assets
results in reduced trust scores and may trigger governance alerts.
